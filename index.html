Privacy Policy

Cadence - Recurring Task Tracker

Overview

Cadence is designed with your privacy in mind. The app stores all your data locally on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

Data Storage

All data you create in Cadence, including:

  • Task sets and tasks
  • Notes and completion history
  • Your preferences and settings

is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure on-device storage. Your data is included in your regular device backups (iCloud or local) if you have backups enabled.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Cadence:

  • Does not require an account or sign-in
  • Does not use analytics or tracking
  • Does not transmit data to any servers
  • Does not include any third-party SDKs
  • Does not display advertisements

Permissions

Cadence may request permission for local notifications to remind you about due tasks. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and no notification data is sent anywhere.

Data Deletion

You can delete any of your data at any time within the app. Uninstalling the app removes all associated data from your device.
